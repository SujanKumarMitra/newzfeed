Welcome to Newzfeed!

The goal of Newzfeed is to give you the absolute best news sources for any topic!
Our topics are carefully curated and constantly updated as we know
the web moves fast so we designed an application that collects relevant news articles from reputed sources from around the world and displays them to our users with a single click of a button.
Our application also classifies these articles into different categories.
